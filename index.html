About Me

Nana Ioseliani AKA nana.ios is a multidisciplinary producer and musician based in Berlin. Drawing from her diverse musical background and a deep love for experimentation, Nana has developed her solo project into an exploration of sound and art.

Her early exposure to Traditional Polyphonic Singing profoundly influenced her understanding of the voice as a powerful instrument of self-expression. Her passion for music expanded beyond singing, leading her to explore various art forms and mediums, including dance, film and fashion.

Nana assumes complete independence as she writes, records, and produces her own music. Her work extends beyond traditional music production, encompassing a wide range of mediums such as imagery, mixed media, and sonic landscapes. From crafting costumes to directing her own music videos, Nana embraces a multidisciplinary approach, pushing the boundaries of artistic expression.
Her debut album, "The Way of Joy," masterfully combines synth, dream, and experimental pop genres, guiding listeners on a journey of self-discovery. The album is like sparkly light bulbs wrapped in a candy dust of magical tales.
Her latest single, "Unknown," fuses hyper-pop and experimental dark pop, creating ethereal soundscapes.
Her upcoming single will be released in early spring.
